About Us

Overcast was formed in 2020 by two sets of barbershopping siblings: Erin and Kate Butler, and Ryan and Jakob Reid. Since forming, Overcast has competed at various BHA regional competitions and recently attended their first BHA national competition. Here they took out the BHA national youth mixed gold medal as well as coming 5th place in the open competition. Looking towards the future, Overcast hopes to sharpen their performance skills, with a dream of one day attending the international BHS varsity quartet contest.
