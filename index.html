About Us

Overcast is an Australian mixed barbershop quartet, formed in 2020 by two sets of siblings that shared a love for barbershop singing and an equal terror of performing in front of an audience.

Looking to the future, we want to continue improving our singing and performing skills to hopefully one day share our music on the international BHS stage!
